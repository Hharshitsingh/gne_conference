Call-For-Papers

Prospective authors are invited to submit the full length papers (MS word file) maximum 10 pages, relevant to the themes of the conference, online at following link. The format of the paper is available at the conference webpage.

The paper(s) must reach the conference secretariat by the due date (February 15, 2022). All papers will be examined through Turnitin or iThentikate to detect potential plagiarism issues.

Format of the paper

Materials Today Proceedings : Under Review

  • Visit the website Materials Today: Proceedings https://www.journals.elsevier.com/materials-today-proceedings
  • Create an account by choosing “Submit your manuscript.”
  • Login with your username and password to https://www.journals.elsevier.com/materials-today-proceedings
  • Click on the Select Submit new manuscript.
  • From the “Choose Article Type” from the dropdown menu and select ICTAAEM-22.
  • Submit and upload your formatted paper (according to the Materials Today: Proceedings (Scopus Index).
  • Confirm your submission
  • Please fill the Registration form available online after receiving intimation regarding the acceptance of your paper.
