Topics of Interest

Track 1. Advances in Materials, Manufacturing Technology, Modeling, Analysis and Optimization

  • Ferrous and Non-ferrous Materials, Composites, Micro/Nano Materials, Ceramics, Polymeric Materials.

  • Functional Materials, Biomaterials, Smart/Intelligent Materials

  • Materials Applications, Mechanical Behavior & Fracture, Performance and Life Cycle.

  • Materials Property and Characterization.

  • Sensors and Actuators.

  • Energy Storage Materials, Heating and Cooling Systems with Renewable Energy and Energy Harvesting.

  • Quantum Materials and Metamaterials.

  • Piezoelectrics and Piezomagnetics.

  • Conventional and Non-conventional machining processes and their Hybridization.

  • Advanced finishing Processes, Micro-Machining.

  • Surface Engineering/Coatings.

  • Material Forming & Joining.

  • Material Design and development.

  • Microwave Processing of Materials.

  • Laser Machining and Sintering.

  • Bio-Manufacturing, Additive Manufacturing.

  • Modelling, Analysis and Simulation of Manufacturing Processes.

  • Multi-objective optimization, Applications of Mathematical Modelling and Optimization Theory and Methods in various areas.

  • Vibration, Noise Analysis and Control.

  • Tribology and wear, Interfaces and Interphases.

  • Creep and Aggressive Environment, Corrosion.

  • Medical Implant Materials, Silica and Carbon Materials.

  • Graphite, Graphene, Magnetic Materials, Optical Materials.

  • Liquid Crystals, Nanotechnology, Batteries, Solar cells.

  • Organic Electronics, Mesoporous Materials, Biocatalysis.

  • Photocatalysis, Polymers.

Track 2. Architecture and Infrastructural Design Materials

  • Architecture and Urban Planning.

  • Climate control in Buildings, Trends of Environmental Technology in Residential Structures.

  • Fatigue in large Constructions.

  • Stability and Strength of Concrete, Steel and Glass Structures and Technology.

  • Hydraulics and Maritime Technology.

  • Bridge design and Construction, Durability and Sustainability of Materials.

  • Advanced Material Characterization Techniques and Damage Mechanics.

  • Multi-scale Modelling of Materials, Advanced Textiles.

  • Multicultural Architecture in the Urban Landscape.

  • Evaluating Design in Municipal Structures.

  • Use of Waste Materials in Infrastructure Projects.

  • Building Information Modelling.

  • Coal Combustion Residual Landfills, Construction and Demolition Waste Landfills, Construction and Environmental Issues

  • Design of New Materials for Sustainability, Eco-efficient and Ultra-High-Performance Cementitious Materials

  • Geotechnical Reuse of Solid Waste, Green and Sustainable Environmental Management

  • Waste Water Treatment processes.

Track 3. Aspects of Materials in Electrical, Electronics and Computer Systems

  • Materials for Sustainability with Technology Shrinking.

  • Fabrication Process of Nanomaterials and Nanodevices, MEMS Integration, 3D Printing, 4D Printing.

  • Wearable Antennas, Materials for Semiconductor Devices, Optoelectronic Materials, Memristors and Memristive Circuits.

  • Sensors and Instrumentation, High-Performance Computing.

  • Integrated Circuits for Communication Applications, Analog and Digital Integrated Circuit Design.

  • Advanced Electronic Circuits and Devices, RF Circuits, Quantum Spintronics.

  • Materials for Energy Harvesting, Electric Power Deregulation and Restructuring, Smart Grids.

  • Renewable and Distributed Generation, Electrical Machines and Drives, Electric Vehicles.

  • Energy Storage, High Voltage Engineering, Electrical Safety.

  • Materials for State-of-the-Art Transistors.

  • Data Mining, Cloud Computing, IoT, Industrial IoT.

  • Machine Learning and Applications, Optimization, Intelligent Transport Systems.

  • High-Performance Computer Networks, Distributed Computing, Adhoc Networks.

  • Human-Computer Interaction, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Big Data Analytics.

  • Augmented Reality, Supply Chain Management, Quality and Inventory control, Logistic management.

  • Material Planning, Warehousing, Procurement.

  • Material Transportation, Disposal and Control, Material Requirement Planning.
